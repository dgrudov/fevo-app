Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Gruvio ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Gruvio mobile and web application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: your name, email address, and password when you register
  • Phone number: collected during onboarding for account security and to reduce fake or duplicate accounts. Your phone number is never shown publicly or shared with other users.
  • Profile data: profile photo, bio, city, date of birth, gender, username, and interests you choose to add
  • Content: events you create, messages you send, photos you upload, and ratings you give
  • Usage data: events you join or attend, buddy connections you make
  • Push notification tokens: stored if you grant notification permission, used only to send you relevant notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Gruvio service
  • To show your profile and events to other users
  • To match you with events based on your interests and city
  • To send push notifications about new events and activity relevant to you
  • To allow other users to connect with you as buddies

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is stored in the EU region. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your profile, events, and activity are visible to other Gruvio users as part of the core functionality of the app. Your phone number is never visible to other users and is used solely for account integrity. You control what appears on your public profile.

5. Photos

Photos you upload to events or your profile are stored securely and visible to other users of the app. You can delete your photos at any time from within the app.

6. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, we may send you push notifications about new events matching your interests, buddy activity, and weekend event digests. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from your profile settings. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, events, messages, and all associated data from our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

Gruvio is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Gruvio after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or want to request deletion of your data, contact us at support@gruvio.app.
